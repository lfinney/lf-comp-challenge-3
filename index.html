    Champion Snapshot

    Role

    Marksman, Fighter

    Position

    Top, Bot, Jungle

    Core Items

    Duskblade, Youmuu's Ghostblade, Infinity Edge, Static Shiv

    Keystone

    Thunderlord's Decree

    Difficulty

    Moderate

    Strategy

    Quinn excels at 1-vs-1 matchups. This gives her a strong presence in the top lane where her opponents are most likely to be melee. Quinn is suspectible to crowd-control effects and since one of her abilities puts her right into melee range, she is not the strongest of team fighters. However, her strong 1-vs-1 abilitiy combined with her utimate, Behind Enemy Lines, makes her an excellent split-pusher who can quickly move around the map.

    Synergies

    Leona, Thresh, Tahm Kench, Shen, Zilean
